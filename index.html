HOW I WORK ?

Ideation

Quality apps demand quality creative input. I guide my clients through the ideation process from research to market validation. Has it been done before? What makes this idea special? How can we take it to the next level?


Defining the Scope

Every Android project needs a roadmap, whether the starting point is an idea or a pre-existing app.
I work together with my clients to define each part of the mobile app development process. That means defining feature sets, deciding on platforms, and ensuring the app can ship within the required timeframe.


UI/UX Design

With a solid plan in place I get to work drawing up wireframes and mapping user experience (UX). My user interface (UI) designers pick it up from there to create the graphical elements, branding assets, and style guide.


Development

At this point I step in to bring your vision to life. Setting up the databases, core logic, and dynamic screen behaviors ensures a fast, seamless, and immersive experience for your users.I meticulously test the Android app throughout the development process to ensure speedy and bug-free mobile app experience. A thorough QA test of app performance and functionality takes place before submitting to the App Store.


Launch

Submitting an Android app to Google Play is an art form in itself. I work closely with my clients to ensure that marketing material, graphics, and app assets are ready for public launch. Android apps submitted to the Google Play app store are usually available to the public within a few hours of being submitted.
